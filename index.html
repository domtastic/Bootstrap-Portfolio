About

Hi, my name is Dominic and I am an inspiring full stack web developer currently enrolled in the University of Denver Ful-Stack Web Dev program. I have decided to change careers as I would like to challenge myself more every day. WHat I really lke about programming is that there is always something new to learn, and every problems can be solved in different ways. I have ambitions to create world changing applications that are engaging and have a positive impact on people's lives.

I am originally from Grand Rapids, Michigan, and went to Michigan State University. I moved to Colorado in 2013 mainly because of my love of the mountains. I am an avid outdoorsman that includes climbing (ice and rock), snowboarding, hiking and backpacking. I recently purchased a house in Denver with my son to be wife, Lindsay, and plan on staying around for a very long time.
